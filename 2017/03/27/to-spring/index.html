A Journey Through Time

Welcome to my latest poem, where words weave a tapestry of emotions and imagery. Enjoy the journey!

The Poem

A Journey Through Time

In the whisper of the morning breeze,
I hear the echoes of ancient seas.
Time dances on the edge of light,
Painting shadows in the flight.

Chorus of the Stars

Stars above, in silent grace,
Guide the traveler through time and space.
With every step, a story unfolds,
In the heart of the brave and bold.

Reflections

Mirrors of the past and future,
Hold the dreams we dare to nurture.
In the river of endless streams,
We sail on boats of hopes and dreams.
